Computer Engine / AI
  • With Board Analysis checked the computer engine does neither move white nor black pieces.
  • By unchecking Board Analysis the next move is on you followed by computer engine continuing playing the other side.
  • Hint: The Board Analysis mode can be used to have two human players play with each other.
  • You are viewing the board from this player's side.
  • In the still running game on change you toggle opponent's view and own view.
  • Just on starting a new game you will be playing the selected color's side.
